Welcome to Branch Saga, the game of my adult life. Take control of me through three levels to discover my latest announcement.

  • Use the left and right arrow key to move in either direction.
  • Hold the down arrow key to fall through a platform or slope.
  • Press space to jump.
  • Collect all the coins in each level to hear more of the story.

You will need to play this on a PC, not phone or tablet - sorry!
